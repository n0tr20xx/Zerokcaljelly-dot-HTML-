    About

However, once I left high school, I didn't do so that often due to other aspects of my life taking over (mainly nutrition, athletics and a very stressful university life). Losing that spark was quite sad, but as of late, I feel that it has been rekindled. I will attempt to express my photography interests in this website and try to describe each one to tell a better story.
